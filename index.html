Genshin Impact is an open world, action-packed, chinese fantasy game developed by miHoYoTM (now re-branded as HOYOVERSE)

The game starts with us being with our sibling, suddenly when an unknown powerful god comes in and attacks us..
We are given a choice among the siblings to choose as our playable character. After getting our other sibling abducted(yea the god is hella powerful 🙂) we are sent to some other world where we wake up and then the game starts..
Long story short... We are on the adventure of finding our other lost sibling. On this journey, we encounter various nations and their respective elements.
Being an anomaly, we can harness the power of all the elements whereas in the Teyvat (the world we fell into) only a single element can be harnessed by a single person. This is known as Vision.

Whilst our jouney, we meet lot of new people with different visions and go through a lot of adventures..

The game consists of 7 elements as

Anemo is the wind element. This is the element for the Mondstat, nation of the winds, which also happens to our first nation of the game.

Pyro is the fire element. Natlan is the nation for pyro. This nation is yet to come in 4.8 update of the game upcoming on 16th of July

Cryo is the ice element. Snezhnaya, the cryo nation is also yet to come in the game..

Geo is the rock element. Liyue, also known as the nation of contracts is the nation for this element

Hydro is the water element. Fontaine, the nation of justice, is the nation for this element.

Electro element belongs to the nation of eternity, namely Inazuma

Dendro element belongs to Sumeru, the nation of knowledge.

Each of these elements have an Archon , who basically is the God of that element.

  Venti is the archon for the anemo element. He is rarely seen in the city of Mondstat and many people often don't know about Venti being the archon.

Zhongli is the archon for the geo element. He lives in Liyue itself, but no one knows he is the (or used to be 🙃) archon.

Raiden Shogun is the electro archon in the game. The idea of eternity is of hers and is one of the strongest archons of the game.

Nahida is the dendro archon. Be wary of her knowledge, she might look like a child but is 500 years old..

Furina is the so-called archon. She has a deep lore behind the question of whether she is the archon or not. 😐

Since other regions are yet to come.. we do not know much about the respective archons

This however does not mean that other characters are not worth paying attention. Supports also play an important role in building a proper team. Some of the strongest supports in the game right now are..
